How do people age with dignity?

Section 1: Design for Ageing

This is where my architectural track and design career starts. Not because it represents old systems, but because it provides the cognitive, architectural, and ergonomic foundation for all spatial realities.

Core Explored Themes

  • Accessibility & spatial navigation
  • Cognition and dementia support structures
  • Ergonomics & physical human factors
  • Emotional well-being & transitional safety
  • Adaptive micro-environments

Evidence Artifacts

Old Age Home Thesis Portfolio, HWWE Ergonomics Framework, and the Adaptive Bed Design Space. Further iterations and metrics can be found referenced directly in the documentation within done_portfolio.pdf and portfolio_oldagehome.pptx.

What stayed with me:
Designing spaces for physical vulnerability completely eliminates room for aesthetic indulgence. When tracking light requirements or modifying handrails for cognitive reassurance, the architecture stops acting as a container and begins acting directly as care.
