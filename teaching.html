Studio Pedagogy Philosophy

Teaching & Curriculum Design

The Design Studio as an Empirical Testing Ground

My teaching philosophy coordinates foundational design principles directly with data-driven construction metrics. Moving past purely visual configurations, I guide students to run design studios as research platforms—where hands-on making, material prototyping loops, and parametric equations help validate spatial performance.

01 // Thinking Through Hands

Encouraging deep material testing through model making, physical joints, and tangible structural iterations.

02 // Computational Logic

Integrating parametric scripting, geometric modulation, and modular system logic into undergraduate studio tracks.

03 // Building Performance

Teaching design students to run empirical simulations, solid-to-void indexing, and energy audit analysis.
