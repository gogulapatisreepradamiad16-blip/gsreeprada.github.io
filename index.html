Professional Practice

The Inquiry

How can commercial design frameworks apply research-driven parameters—specifically adaptive reuse strategies, human factors, and regional material chains—to physical spaces and digital products?

Projects & Selected Works

A compilation of direct industry executions, physical architecture coordination, and spatial analysis layouts across multiple design scales:

  • Interior Design & Styling: Direct development and styling of customized spatial layouts, balancing human requirements with materials procurement execution paths.
  • Adaptive Reuse Schemes: Spatial retrofitting models and renovation frameworks focused on transforming outdated structures using sustainable architecture intervention criteria.
  • Furniture Design: Drafting and building custom interior furniture fixtures modeled specifically around user lifestyle transitions and structural assembly.
  • Spatial Analysis: Technical field mapping and contextual planning utilizing QGIS vector tracking engines.
  • Freelance Residential Projects: Execution of localized spatial planning blueprints, millwork details, material schedules, and production presentations.

Design for Ageing

The Inquiry

How can environmental psychology, ergonomic modeling, and spatial architecture protect human dignity, autonomy, and universal accessibility in living spaces built for aging populations experiencing physical transition?

Projects & Selected Works

This path forms the architectural and human-centered foundation of my design logic, bridging structural systems with qualitative health tracking to define parameters for changing lifespans.

  • Senior Care Complex Thesis: Spatial planning architectural parameters mapping environmental frameworks for senior communities.
  • Qualitative Bathing Disability Research: Fieldwork and user assessment evaluating environmental hazards and dependencies experienced by elderly cohorts in Hyderabad.
  • Mechanically Adaptive Bed Frame & Furniture: Prototyping responsive interior nursing bed hardware built to match geriatric dressing and rehabilitation needs.

Craft Futures

The Inquiry

How do historic craft traditions navigate the pressures of raw material scarcity, industrial automation, and rapid market migration without sacrificing their authentic tacit knowledge or cultural heritage?

Projects & Selected Works

A multi-year longitudinal field study balancing local ethnographic tracking with design interventions across distinct regional craft ecologies:

  • Kondapalli Materials Resilience: Investigating product diversification, alternative supply loops, and wood-substitution configurations to address systemic forestry shortages.
  • Nakashi Mechanical Integration: Analyzing spatial and operational friction points between human artisanal hand-carving systems and computational CNC machining paths.
  • Kalamkari Fabric Media Transitions: Mapping the shifting boundaries of traditional pen methods, regional motifs, and modern block/screen translations.

Fabrication & Innovation

The Inquiry

How can digital fabrication processes merge with local, vernacular architectural building systems to form a collaborative design language that balances standard industrial precision with organic site-specific customization?

Projects & Selected Works

The engineering core of my structural design research, integrating material science with physical prototyping loops to build adaptive construction components.

  • A Modular Architectural Screen System: Engineering geometric screen assemblies (Jaalis) utilizing custom interlocking properties to achieve site-specific light filtering.
  • Gear-Actuated Flexure Casting: Prototyping variable mechanical mold configurations capable of continuous localized material thickness modifications.
  • System For Forming Three-Dimensional Structures: Designing modular structural block interlocking frameworks to form complex spatial screens without standard tool dependencies.

Narrative Design

The Inquiry

How can visual frameworks and storytelling structures translate early developmental concepts into media that support childhood literacy and behavioral transition landmarks?

Commercialized Products & Selected Works

An exploration of publication media acting as cognitive learning frameworks. This practice combines targeted early-childhood text authorship with stylized book architectures.

  • ANAGHA’S SHINY LITTLE TEETH (2025): Published children's book mapping health routines, sensory transitions, and somatic awareness milestones.
  • Anagha Goes to School: Story structure and text layout addressing early childhood separation anxieties, behavioral adaptation, and classroom transitions.
  • Pippa is the Boss of Her Body: Book architecture, typography layout, and graphic design mapping early personal agency, boundaries, and safety concepts.

Research

Theoretical Frameworks & Deep Interests

Theoretical Position

Elastic Vernaculars

An architectural position asserting that traditional regional building knowledge must not remain frozen as an historical artifact. Instead, it must serve as an elastic parameters framework, adapting to contemporary computational logic and resource changes without fracturing its underlying structural intent.

Conceptual Framework

The Fabricational Third Space

The boundary zone where automated, parametric CNC tools intersect with the contextual, variable intuition of human craftsmanship. This framework balances machine precision with human craft variations, creating a synthesis of digital accuracy and artisan authenticity.

Methodological Contribution

IMMA-DBR

An integrated, multi-scalar methodological loop combining systemic site analysis with Design-Based Research (DBR). This structure supports rigorous, cyclic transitions between abstract conceptual theory and empirical, field-tested prototype performance.
